Neon Glow Sequin Fabric for Rave Wear, Cosplay & Festival Fashion – 12mm

Overview
Neon glow sequin fabric features large 12mm sequins that create bold, eye-catching effects. The bright neon colors make this fabric perfect for rave wear, festival fashion, cosplay costumes, and statement garments. Each sequin reflects light to create stunning visual impact that stands out under stage lights and blacklights.

The fabric base is lightweight and flexible, making it easy to sew and work with for costuming and fashion applications. As a leading fabric manufacturer in China, Textilenova Textile offers custom colors, sizes, and sequin specifications to meet your design requirements.
Customizable
