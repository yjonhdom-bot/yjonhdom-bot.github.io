About Ruzhou Yunfan Textile

Yunfan Textile is a factory-direct manufacturer of sequin fabrics, embroidery fabrics, and decorative textiles, based in Ruzhou City, Henan Province, China. Established in 2016, we have built over 10 years of production and export experience, supplying wholesale buyers across 80+ countries.

Our 1,540m2 production facility operates 20 production lines, producing 30,000+ meters per day. Every order is inspected before shipment.

What We Manufacture

  • Sequin Fabrics - 3mm, 5mm, 7mm, 18mm; stretch mesh, flat woven, reversible flip sequin
  • Embroidery Fabrics - 3D embroidery, beading, diamond, floral lace
  • Sequin Home Textiles - table runners, tablecloths, backdrops, cushion covers
  • Functional Polyester Fabrics - antibacterial, antimicrobial, moisture-wicking

500+ active styles in stock. OEM/ODM and Pantone color matching available.

Why Buyers Choose Yunfan Textile

  • Factory Direct - No middlemen, manufacturer pricing
  • Low MOQ - From 50 yards per color/style
  • Free Samples - Stock samples free, buyer pays shipping
  • Fast Turnaround - Stock 3-5 days, custom 15-25 days
  • Quality Control - 100% inspection before shipment, 2-year warranty
  • Global Shipping - DHL, FedEx, UPS, sea freight to 80+ countries
  • 24h Response - Reply to all inquiries within 24 hours
